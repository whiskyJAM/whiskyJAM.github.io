Support & Help

Welcome to the support page for LETTERMOLE! Here, you’ll find answers to common questions, troubleshooting tips, and ways to contact us for assistance.

How do I use LETTERMOLE?

Open the app and navigate through the main menu to select your desired game mode:

Daily Challenge:

  • A unique challenge every day.
  • You only get one chance to play for a specific day—make it count!
  • Complete daily challenges to win valuable rewards that can be used in-game.

Unlimited:

  • Enjoy endless games with a new, random bank of letters every time.
  • This mode is perfect for honing your skills.

For detailed game statistics, tap the star icon on the main menu. You’ll find the following data for each game mode:

  • Total score from all games combined.
  • Total number of games played.
  • Total LETTERMOLE!s achieved.
  • Your high score for each game mode.
  • Number of words made for each word length.
  • Your longest word.
  • Total score for the current month’s Daily Challenges.
  • Your best month and its total score for Daily Challenges.

Achieve a LETTERMOLE! by clearing the gameboard of all letters. Doing so rewards you with a score bonus and additional in-game rewards!

For in-depth instructions on gameplay mechanics, visit the ‘How to Play’ section (tap the question mark icon on the main menu).

Why is my score not saving?

  • Verify that your device has sufficient storage space.
  • Restart the app.
  • If the issue persists, please contact us for assistance.

The app isn’t opening or keeps crashing.

  • Ensure your device is running the latest iOS version.
  • Force close the app and try reopening it.
  • If the problem continues, uninstall and reinstall the app (note: your data may be reset).
